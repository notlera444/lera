Children attending school is vital for their overall development.

life skills, foster social interaction, and provide a structured environment.

thinking abilities, and emotional intelligence. Additionally, school promotes
discipline, teamwork, and a sense of responsibility, preparing kids for future challenges.
Overall, it lays a foundation for lifelong learning and personal growth.
